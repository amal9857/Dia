### HOW TO ACTIVATE REAL EMAILS (Free)

To send real verification codes to your Gmail, we need to connect a free email service. I cannot do this automatically because it requires your account permission.

**Please follow these 4 steps (Takes 2 minutes):**

1.  **Sign Up**: Go to **[https://www.emailjs.com/](https://www.emailjs.com/)** and create a free account.
2.  **Add Service**:
    *   Click "Email Services" -> "Add New Service".
    *   Select **Gmail**.
    *   Click "Connect Account" (Sign in with the Gmail you want to send FROM).
    *   Click "Create Service".
    *   **COPY the "Service ID"** (e.g., `service_xyz`).

3.  **Add Template**:
    *   Click "Email Templates" -> "Create New Template".
    *   **Subject**: `Your Verification Code`
    *   **Content**: `Hello {{to_email}}, Your code is: {{verification_code}}`
    *   Click "Save".
    *   **COPY the "Template ID"** (e.g., `template_abc`).

4.  **Get Public Key**:
    *   Click on your Name/Avatar in the top-right -> "Dashboard" or "Account".
    *   Look for "Public Key" (e.g., `user_123xyz`).
    *   **COPY the Public Key**.
